Expected Format

One top-bottom stereo cubemap strip. Top half is the left eye, bottom half is the right eye. Each half must be a 6x1 strip of square faces.

Example: 18432 x 6144 -> two rows of 18432 x 3072, face size 3072 x 3072.

VR Instructions

Quest 3 Usage

  • Open this app in Meta Quest Browser over HTTPS or localhost.
  • Upload a stereo cubemap strip or use the demo placeholder.
  • Press Enter VR and allow immersive mode.
  • Press B on the right Quest controller to show or hide the floating VR menu.
  • Use the right controller trigger to click the floating VR menu when it is visible.
  • Hold trigger away from the menu, or hold grip, then drag left/right to rotate the stereo cubemap.
